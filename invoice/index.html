When to choose invoice

Invoice purchase makes sense when finance runs the buy, procurement has rules, or you'd rather pay once annually than watch twelve card charges. It's also the right path when your security or vendor-review process needs to run before commitment.

  • Pro — annual invoice. For agencies, contractors, and small teams that self-scope and want one line item on the books.
  • Operator — proposal-led. For multi-user teams and implementation-heavy rollouts. We scope before we quote; quote becomes an annual invoice on approval.
  • Enterprise — custom terms. For procurement-heavy orgs. MSA, SOW, and DPA are part of the proposal package, not bolt-ons after the fact.
