Why people need it

Most businesses are not failing from lack of effort. They are slowing down from disconnected systems.

When leads, follow-up, workflow, and reporting live in different places, things get missed.

That usually looks like delayed responses, dropped handoffs, unclear ownership, inconsistent follow-up, and wasted effort trying to figure out what is going on.

SourceDeck makes those problems easier to see, so you can fix them before they become expensive.
